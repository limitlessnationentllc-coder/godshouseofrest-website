FAQ — Church Near San Diego South Bay

What is the best church in South Bay San Diego?

God's House of Rest in Chula Vista serves the entire South Bay with Sunday worship, prayer, discipleship, and family-focused ministry at 618 E Street Suite H, Chula Vista, CA 91910.

Is there a church in South Bay San Diego for young families?

Yes — God's House of Rest has active children's, youth, men's, and women's ministries specifically designed for South Bay families.

What areas does God's House of Rest Church serve?

We serve Chula Vista, National City, Bonita, Eastlake, Otay Ranch, Imperial Beach, and the greater South Bay San Diego region.

What denomination is God's House of Rest Church?

We are a non-denominational Christian church. Our focus is the Bible, Matthew 11:28, and building disciples — not religious tradition.

How can I connect with God's House of Rest Church in South Bay?

Visit us Sunday at 10 AM at 618 E Street Suite H, Chula Vista, CA 91910 — or call (619) 931-9344. We'd love to meet you.
