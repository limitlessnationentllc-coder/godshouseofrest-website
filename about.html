Our Story

More Than a Sunday Service

God's House of Rest Church was born from a simple conviction held by Pastor R. Tyrone Robertson and First Lady Deborah Robertson: every person carries something — a wound, a question, a burden they were never meant to carry alone. And Jesus meant every word when He said, "Come to Me… and I will give you rest."

Together, the Robertsons planted this church in Chula Vista, California because this community deserves a church that shows up. Not a church that performs on Sundays and disappears on Monday — but a living, breathing family that walks alongside people through the real stuff of life.

We are disciples, not attenders. We are family, not spectators. We believe transformation is possible, healing is real, and no one is too far gone to find rest in God.

What We Believe

Our Mission in Four Words

Everything we do comes back to four core convictions about what church is supposed to be.

01
Rest
In a world that never stops, we offer something countercultural: stillness, peace, and the presence of God. "Come to Me… and I will give you rest." — Matthew 11:28
02
Healing
We believe the church should be the most healing place on earth. We make room for broken people because we are broken people — and God specializes in restoration.
03
Discipleship
We don't create attenders — we create disciples. Faith is not a spectator sport, and we commit to walking alongside every person on their journey with God.
04
Community
We are family. The kind that shows up, sits together, prays together, and celebrates together. Church is not a building — it's the people inside it.

Common Questions

FAQ About Our Church

What kind of church is Gods House of Rest?

Gods House of Rest is a non-denominational Christian church in Chula Vista, CA focused on rest, healing, prayer, and discipleship. Every ministry is anchored to Matthew 11:28.

Who leads Gods House of Rest Church?

Pastor R. Tyrone Robertson and First Lady Deborah Robertson lead the church. Pastor Tyrone preaches most Sundays. First Lady Deborah leads Womens Ministry. Meet the team

What does Gods House of Rest believe?

We believe in the authority of the Bible, the Trinity, salvation through Jesus Christ, baptism, prayer, and the local church as the body of Christ. Non-denominational and Christ-centered.

Is Gods House of Rest welcoming to newcomers?

Absolutely. No dress code, no religious resume required. Come as you are. Our welcome team greets you at the door and helps you find your place.

How long has Gods House of Rest been in Chula Vista?

Gods House of Rest has been serving the Chula Vista and South Bay community for over a decade, building disciples and serving families across the region.
