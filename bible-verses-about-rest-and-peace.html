15 Bible Verses About Rest and Peace for Weary Souls

By the God's House of Rest team Reviewed by Pastor R. Tyrone Robertson Updated June 18, 2026 6 min read

The best Bible verses about rest and peace remind us of one steady truth: we were never meant to carry it all alone. Whether you're anxious, exhausted, or just longing for quiet, these 15 Scriptures speak peace to a weary soul — each with a short reflection and a link to read it in full. Start with the one your heart needs most.

Start Here
  • Most beloved verse on rest: Matthew 11:28.
  • For anxiety: Philippians 4:6–7 and 1 Peter 5:7.
  • For a peaceful night: Psalm 4:8.

Bible verses about rest

1. Matthew 11:28–30

"Come to Me, all you who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest." The verse our whole church is named for — a personal invitation to trade striving for rest. Read it → (and here's what Matthew 11:28 means).

2. Psalm 23:1–3

"He makes me lie down in green pastures. He leads me beside still waters. He restores my soul." A picture of a God who slows us down on purpose. Read it →

3. Exodus 33:14

"My presence will go with you, and I will give you rest." Rest isn't only a place we reach — it's a Person we walk with. Read it →

4. Psalm 62:1

"For God alone my soul waits in silence; from him comes my salvation." Sometimes rest looks like simply being still before God. Read it →

5. Hebrews 4:9–10

"So then, there remains a Sabbath rest for the people of God." A reminder that rest is a gift built into God's design for us. Read it →

Bible verses about peace

6. Philippians 4:6–7

"Do not be anxious about anything… and the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts." The antidote to anxiety is prayer, not pretending. Read it →

7. John 14:27

"Peace I leave with you; my peace I give to you… Let not your hearts be troubled." A peace the world can't manufacture and can't take away. Read it →

8. Isaiah 26:3

"You keep him in perfect peace whose mind is stayed on you." Peace follows trust, like calm follows an anchor. Read it →

9. Psalm 4:8

"In peace I will both lie down and sleep; for you alone, O Lord, make me dwell in safety." A verse to pray on sleepless nights. Read it →

10. 1 Peter 5:7

"Casting all your anxieties on him, because he cares for you." You're not bothering God with your worries — He invites them. Read it →

How to actually find rest in these verses

Verses calm us most when we don't just read them but pray them. Bring the words to God honestly — here's a simple guide to how to start praying if you're new to it. If your weariness runs deep, you may also find help in our piece on faith and burnout or finding hope when life feels heavy. And you don't have to do it alone — our team would be glad to pray with you.

A Prayer for Peace

"Lord, quiet my racing mind tonight. Thank You that Your peace doesn't depend on everything being fixed. I give You what I'm carrying, and I trust You to keep me. Help me lie down and rest, knowing You never sleep. Amen."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best Bible verse about rest?

Matthew 11:28 — "Come to Me, all you who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest." It captures Jesus' personal invitation to bring our exhaustion to Him and receive rest for the soul.

What does the Bible say about peace?

Peace is a gift from God that surpasses understanding (Philippians 4:7), rooted in trusting Him (Isaiah 26:3). Jesus gives a peace the world cannot give (John 14:27), so our hearts need not be troubled.

What verses help with anxiety and a restless mind?

Philippians 4:6–7 (pray instead of worry), 1 Peter 5:7 (cast your anxieties on Him), and Psalm 4:8 (lie down and sleep in peace).

How do I find rest in God?

Bring your burdens to Him honestly in prayer, trust His promises, and stay connected through Scripture, worship, and community. It's less about doing more and more about releasing what you were never meant to carry alone.
