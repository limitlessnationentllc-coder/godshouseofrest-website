Pastoral Leadership

Servants First

Pastor Tyrone and First Lady Deborah Robertson don't just lead a church — they live the message. Every Sunday, every prayer, every conversation in the hallway reflects the same heart: that every person who walks through our doors deserves to be seen, loved, and pointed to rest.

Senior Pastor

Pastor R. Tyrone Robertson

Pastor Tyrone Robertson is the founding shepherd of God's House of Rest Church in Chula Vista, CA. Called to ministry with a burning conviction that the Church should be a refuge — not a performance — Pastor Tyrone has spent years building a community where people encounter God's presence and leave transformed, not just inspired.

His preaching is direct, scripture-anchored, and deeply pastoral. He believes discipleship is built one honest conversation at a time, and that the best sermon a church can preach is the way it treats the person walking through the door for the first time.

"Come to Me, all you who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest." Matthew 11:28 — Anchor Verse

First Lady · Women's Ministry

First Lady Deborah Robertson

First Lady Deborah Robertson is a pillar of God's House of Rest — a teacher, intercessor, and encourager whose presence shapes the culture of the entire church. She leads Women's Ministry with a gift for creating space where women feel safe to be honest about their struggles and bold about their faith.

First Lady Deborah carries a particular heart for women walking through seasons of transition, loss, and healing. She believes that when women are strengthened, families are strengthened — and when families are strengthened, communities change.

"She is clothed with strength and dignity, and she laughs without fear of the future." Proverbs 31:25
