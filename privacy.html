Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Who We Are

God's House of Rest Church ("we," "our," or "us") is a Christian church located at 618 E Street Suite H, Chula Vista, CA 91910. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through our website at godshouseofrest.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and email address submitted via prayer request forms or contact forms
  • Prayer requests and personal messages
  • Phone number if provided for call-back requests
  • Giving information processed through third-party payment processors

We may also collect non-personal technical data such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages via analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to prayer requests and contact inquiries
  • To send church updates, event announcements, and newsletters (only if you opt in)
  • To process online giving and send donation receipts
  • To improve our website and communications

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Prayer Requests

Prayer requests are treated with the utmost confidentiality. They are read by our pastoral prayer team only and are not shared publicly or used for any purpose other than intercessory prayer.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored using industry-standard security practices. We use reputable third-party services (such as form processors and email platforms) that maintain their own security standards. We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, email us at info@godshouseofrest.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
