Common Questions

FAQ — Sermons and Messages

Where can I watch sermons from Gods House of Rest?

Sermons are available on this page and on our YouTube channel. New messages are posted after every Sunday service.

Who preaches at Gods House of Rest?

Pastor R. Tyrone Robertson is the primary teaching pastor. Scripture-anchored, practical, and pastoral. Learn more about Pastor Robertson

Does Gods House of Rest stream live?

Yes. We stream live on Sundays at 10 AM on YouTube. Check the homepage countdown during service hours for the live link.

How often are new sermons posted?

New sermons are posted weekly after Sunday service. Subscribe on YouTube so you never miss a message.

Can I suggest a sermon topic?

Absolutely. Reach out at info@godshouseofrest.com or speak with Pastor Robertson on a Sunday.
