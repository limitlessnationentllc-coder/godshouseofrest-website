Where Your Gift Goes

Every dollar given to God's House of Rest is a dollar invested in people — not programs.

Community Outreach
Feeding families, supporting neighbors, and being the hands and feet of Jesus throughout Chula Vista and South Bay San Diego.
Youth & Children
Investing in the next generation — curriculum, events, Teen Vibe Ministry, VBS, and safe environments where kids encounter God.
Ministry & Discipleship
Sunday worship, Bible Study, prayer ministry, and the infrastructure that keeps the church doors open and the mission moving forward.
