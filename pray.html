Common Questions

FAQ — Prayer at God's House of Rest

How do I submit a prayer request?

Fill out the form above. Your request goes directly to our pastoral team and is covered in prayer privately and faithfully. You can also call us at (619) 931-9344 or email info@godshouseofrest.com.

Are prayer requests kept private?

Yes. All requests are treated with complete confidentiality by the pastoral team. When you check the "Keep my request private" box, it stays between you and our leaders.

Does the pastoral team actually pray over my request?

Absolutely. Every request submitted is reviewed by Pastor Robertson or a ministry leader and covered in prayer. You are not just submitting a form — you're reaching the heart of a community that believes prayer changes things.

Can I request prayer in person?

Yes — and we encourage it. Come to any Sunday service at 10 AM at 618 E Street Suite H, Chula Vista and speak with a team member or Pastor Robertson directly. We offer prayer at the altar after every service.

What kinds of prayer requests can I submit?

Any and all. Health, relationships, finances, grief, family, addiction, loneliness, fear, purpose — nothing is too big or too small. We believe in a God who hears every prayer.
