Our Mission
We don't create attenders We create disciples.
We don't create spectators We build family.
We don't offer routine We help restore lives.
We don't offer religion We offer rest.

God's House of Rest is more than a church — it's a place of healing, restoration, and purpose for every person who walks through our doors.

Located in the heart of Chula Vista, we believe that everyone is carrying something. A burden. A question. A wound. And we believe Jesus meant every word when He said, "Come to Me… and I will give you rest."

We don't ask you to have it all together. We ask you to come as you are — and we'll walk through it together.
