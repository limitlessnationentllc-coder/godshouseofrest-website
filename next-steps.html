The Growth Track

Four stages. Each one builds on the last. This is how attenders become disciples — and disciples become leaders.

1

Salvation & Baptism

Made a decision to follow Jesus? Baptism is your first public declaration of faith. It's not a requirement for belonging — it's a milestone of commitment. Talk to us on Sunday or reach out anytime.

2

Church Membership

Membership is a covenant — not a club card. It means you're committed to this community and this community is committed to you. It's the difference between a visitor and a family member.

3

Small Group & Bible Study

Sunday is the spark. Small groups are where the fire stays lit. We meet mid-week for Bible study and smaller community gatherings — real conversations, real accountability, real growth.

4

Find Your Place to Serve

Every person at God's House of Rest has a role. Not everyone is a preacher — but everyone is a servant. Find where your gifts meet the needs of the community and step in.

Common Questions

FAQ — Getting Connected

How do I get connected at God's House of Rest?

Start by visiting on a Sunday at 10 AM. After the service, introduce yourself to any team member or fill out a connect card. We'll follow up and help you find your place.

What is the Growth Track?

Our Growth Track walks you through four stages: Salvation and Baptism, Church Membership, Small Group participation, and finding a place to Serve. Each step deepens your roots in the community and in your faith.

How do I get baptized at God's House of Rest?

If you've made a decision to follow Jesus and want to take the step of baptism, contact us at info@godshouseofrest.com or speak with Pastor Robertson on a Sunday. We'll walk you through next steps.

How do I start serving at God's House of Rest?

Reach out to the church office at (619) 931-9344 or info@godshouseofrest.com. We have serving opportunities in worship, hospitality, children's ministry, outreach, and more.

Is there a small group or Bible study I can join?

Yes. We have Bible study and small group gatherings during the week. Ask the team on Sunday or contact us to find one that fits your schedule and season of life.
